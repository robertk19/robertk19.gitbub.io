What is the EWSC all about?

Pushing surfing to the edge

At the extreme weather surfing club, we are all about taking surfing to the extreme! We are always searching for people willing to surf in the gnarliest of weathers and ride the biggest waves. EWSC.Com is the home of all things extreme surfing in Victoria/New South Wales, and features articles published by our club leaders daily.

If you you think you got what it takes, take a look at our newsletter, articles and some of the events we run weekly and see if you are ready to join us!
